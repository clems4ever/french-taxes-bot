# Impot-Bot

Impot-Bot is an open-intent chatbot that helps french people calculate their income taxes. This bot is a showcase of the power of open-intent.

The bot is accessible [here](https://www.facebook.com/simulationimpot/?ref=br_rs). Say 'hello' and the bot will help you with your incomes.

[open-intent] is a framework that helps you create chatbot in minutes with very little effort.
